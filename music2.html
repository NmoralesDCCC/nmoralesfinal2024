ALBUMS

What You Were Meant to Be

"What You Were Meant to Be" was released on June 23rd, 2023 and is my most personal project to date.
It include 9 tracks (12 for deluxe), and features Amato Peace, Connor Gregory & Zoddee.
This project in general tackles my own self-image, trying to find my purpose within me.
It was self-produced by me, including some co-producers like T0ny (Anthony Sands) & BlurredVision (Isiah Ortiz).

The Fall of Paradise

"The Fall of Paradise" was released on March 30th, 2022, marking it as my first studio album.
This album includes 14 tracks (20 for deluxe), and features Lost Mind, Joshie & Valor.
The project is about a failing relationship, trying to cope and process the dim reality.
It was self-produced by me, including some co-producers like
StoneSoWavy, King 80, ONE, Lost Mind, Filament Beatz & H3 Music.

Toxic

"Toxic" was released on February 23rd, 2021 as a mixtape on all platforms.
This album includes 8 tracks (13 for deluxe), and features Valor, Connor Gregory, T0ny & Drvpe.
The project overall isn't story driven, it's more or less a compilation of songs put together.
It was self-produced by me, including some co-producers like T0ny, Cxdy & H3 Music.
